What it's not

Let's be clear.

🚫

Not a bot

It doesn't post for you. It doesn't auto-reply. It doesn't run in the background. You click, you choose, you post. Always.

🚫

Not generating opinions

The ideas are 100% yours. Sleeping Bird doesn't have opinions. It takes yours and helps you phrase them with clarity and punch.

🚫

Not collecting your data

Zero backend. Zero analytics. Zero accounts. Everything runs in your browser with your own API key. Nothing leaves your machine. Period.

Privacy

Your browser. Your key. That's it.

Sleeping Bird has no servers. Your API key is stored locally in your browser's storage. Your drafts, your replies, your data — none of it ever touches our infrastructure, because we don't have any. This isn't a privacy policy checkbox. It's the architecture.

Requires a Grok API key from console.x.ai — free tier available.
